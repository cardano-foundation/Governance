## Summary

The meeting confirmed that the Critical Integrations Budget (CIB) and net change limit (NCL) extension governance actions have already met their approval thresholds. The team aligned on voting for the new Constitutional Committee (CC) member, who won the snap election. The priority is to execute the CC member vote immediately to ensure the governance process remains functional.

## Details

### Current Governance Actions and Approvals

There are four live governance actions at the time of the meeting. The CIB and the NCL extension have already met the necessary approval thresholds, two new actions were submitted - Treasury Withdrawal for the Critical Integrations Budget and Update Constitutional Committee.

### Constitutional Committee (CC) Member Selection

Cardano Curia won the CC snap election. All members aligned on supporting the governance actions to facilitate governance processes for the remainder of the year and 2026.

### Voting Requirements and Timeline

The immediate priority is the vote for the CC member and the NCL extension that would enable the rest of the governance actions to be approved and executed
